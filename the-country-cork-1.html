    the country cork

    ​

         The Country Cork is a beautiful, country inspired indoor/outdoor wine bar and event space located just off of route 29 in beautiful Hunlock Creek, Pa.  It is nestled in the woods near Moon Lake State Recreation Area.

    You can now purchase delicious Pisano Family wines by the bottle at The Country Cork.

    ​

    Our Hours are:  Thurs 3 -8 pm

                              Fri 12-8 pm

                              Sat 12-8 pm

                              Sun 12-5 pm

    ​

         The bar features a hand crafted bar top is made from a tree that grew right on the property and the decor was fashioned from reclaimed wood that once stood on the property also.  The bar features three large screen televisions and a stereo sound system.  The bar is, of course, stocked with delicious Pisano Family Wines.  There is also a table for those that don't want to sit at the bar.

       The outdoor space features a pretty picnic area with fire pits for those brisk nights.  There is plenty of parking for all of your guests.

       The Country cork is the prefect venue for anything from a birthday, anniversary or graduation party to corporate retreats. 

    ​
